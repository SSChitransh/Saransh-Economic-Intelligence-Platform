Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This platform (Economic Intelligence Platform) is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. What data is collected?

None. All interactions – including chat messages and file uploads – are processed locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

2. How is data used?

Data is used only for the immediate purpose of generating responses or analyzing uploaded files. It is not retained after the session ends.

3. Third-party services

This platform uses CDN-hosted libraries (Plotly, Transformers.js, PDF.js, Tesseract.js) for functionality. These services may collect anonymised usage data according to their own policies.

4. Your rights

You have full control over your data – simply close the browser tab to delete any temporary data.

If you have any questions, please contact us via GitHub.
